IT Support Technician - ITT02

As an IT Support Technician, you will provide technical support to clients, troubleshoot hardware and software issues, and ensure the smooth operation of IT systems.

Salary Range:
  • $60,000 to $80,000 annually
Reports To:
  • IT Support Manager
Key Responsibilities:
  1. Identify and resolve issues with computer hardware, software, and networks.
  2. Provide technical assistance to users via phone, email, or in-person.
  3. Install and configure computer systems and applications.
  4. Maintain records of technical issues, solutions, and user interactions.
  5. Assist users with technical issues and provide guidance on IT tools and systems.
Qualifications:
  • Essential:
    • Associate's degree in Information Technology or a related field.
    • 2+ years of experience in IT support or a similar role.
    • Strong knowledge of computer hardware, software, and networking.
    • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Preferable:
    • Certifications such as CompTIA A+ or Network+.
    • Experience with help desk software and remote support tools.
