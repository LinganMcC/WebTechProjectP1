IT Support Technician - ITT02

As an IT Support Technician, you will be responsible for providing technical support to our clients, troubleshooting hardware and software issues, and ensuring the smooth operation of IT systems.

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Identifying and fixing problems with computer hardware, software, networks, and other IT systems.
  2. Addressing inquiries from users via phone, email, or in-person, providing technical assistance and guidance.
  3. Install and configure computer systems and applications.
  4. Assisting users with technical issues, providing guidance, and ensuring they have the necessary tools and knowledge to perform their tasks.
  5. Maintaining records of technical issues, solutions, and user interactions for future reference and analysis.

Qualifications:

  • Associate's degree in Information Technology or related field.
  • Preferred but not needed prior experience in IT support or a similar role.
  • Strong knowledge of computer hardware, software, and networking.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.

Salary Range:

$60,000 to $80,000 annually

Cloud Engineer - CEN19

As a cloud engineer, you are a technical expert who helps companies succeed with cloud-based storage, services, and infrastructure by building, maintaining, provisioning, securing, and optimizing digital systems that operate using cloud technology.

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Design and implement cloud solutions that meet specific business needs.
  2. Develop, deploy, and maintain cloud-native applications and services.
  3. Manage and maintain the cloud infrastructure, including virtual networks, storage, and compute resources.
  4. Troubleshoot issues, optimize performance, and ensure the security and reliability of cloud-based systems.
  5. Collaborate with other IT professionals, such as cloud architects, DevOps engineers, and software engineers.

Qualifications:

  • A bachelor's degree in computer science, information technology, or a related field is a common starting point
  • Preferred prior experience in IT roles, such as systems administration or networking, can also be beneficial.
  • Proficiency in programming languages like Python, Java, Ruby, and C++ is essential for automation, scripting, and application development.
  • Familiarity with major cloud platforms like Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform (GCP) is crucial.
  • A strong understanding of networking concepts, protocols, and security is necessary for managing cloud infrastructure.

Salary Range:

$105,000 to $125,000 annually

Cybersecurity Specialist - CSS44

As a cybersecurity specialist, also known as an information security analyst, your job is to protect computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, theft, or damage by implementing security measures, detecting threats, and responding to security incidents.

Key Responsibilities:

  1. Plan and implement security policies and procedures to protect an organization's IT infrastructure.
  2. Monitor networks and systems for suspicious activity and potential security breaches.
  3. Respond to security incidents, such as malware infections or data breaches, to contain damage and prevent future attacks.
  4. Conduct security assessments and audits to identify vulnerabilities and recommend improvements.
  5. Identify and assess vulnerabilities in systems and networks to determine potential risks.

Qualifications:

  • A master's degree in cybersecurity, computer science, or a related field.
  • Preferred prior experience in cybersecurity or a similar role.
  • Understanding network protocols, firewalls, and intrusion detection systems.
  • Knowledge of encryption and decryption techniques.
  • Familiarity with programming languages like Python

Salary Range:

$133,454 to $190,050 annually [depending on experience.]
