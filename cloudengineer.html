Cloud Engineer - CEN19

As a Cloud Engineer, you will design, implement, and maintain cloud-based systems to support the organization's digital infrastructure and business needs.

Salary Range:
  • $105,000 to $125,000 annually
Reports To:
  • Cloud Solutions Architect
Key Responsibilities:
  1. Design and implement cloud solutions that meet specific business needs.
  2. Develop, deploy, and maintain cloud-native applications and services.
  3. Manage and maintain the cloud infrastructure, including virtual networks, storage, and compute resources.
  4. Troubleshoot issues, optimize performance, and ensure the security and reliability of cloud-based systems.
  5. Collaborate with other IT professionals, such as cloud architects, DevOps engineers, and software engineers.
Qualifications:
  • Essential:
    • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
    • 3+ years of experience in cloud engineering or related roles.
    • Proficiency in programming languages like Python, Java, or Ruby.
    • Familiarity with major cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud Platform.
    • Strong understanding of networking concepts, protocols, and security.
  • Preferable:
    • Certifications such as AWS Certified Solutions Architect or Microsoft Azure Administrator.
    • Experience with containerization tools like Docker or Kubernetes.
