Cybersecurity Specialist - CSS44

As a Cybersecurity Specialist, your role is to safeguard the organization's IT infrastructure by implementing security measures, detecting threats, and responding to incidents.

Salary Range:
  • $133,454 to $190,050 annually
Reports To:
  • Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
Key Responsibilities:
  1. Develop and enforce security policies and procedures.
  2. Monitor networks for suspicious activity and respond to incidents.
  3. Conduct security assessments and audits to identify vulnerabilities.
  4. Implement and maintain firewalls, encryption, and intrusion detection systems.
  5. Provide training to staff on cybersecurity best practices.
Qualifications:
  • Essential:
    • Master's degree in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, or related field.
    • 5+ years of experience in cybersecurity roles.
    • Proficiency in network protocols, firewalls, and intrusion detection systems.
    • Strong knowledge of encryption and decryption techniques.
  • Preferable:
    • Certifications such as CISSP, CEH, or CISM.
    • Experience with Python or other scripting languages.
